# Evidence: driving a snapshot through an S4 gateway pinned to zstd-19 trips
# S4's 30s per-connection read timeout (--read-timeout-seconds, "slowloris
# guard") while compressing a large multipart part, so repository-s3 sees a
# dropped upload and the snapshot completes PARTIAL. This is why high zstd
# levels belong to `s4 recompact` (backend-direct, no client connection), not
# the live snapshot path. Captured 2026-06-18, S4 v1.2.2.
